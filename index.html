Welcome to Concentration, the classic matching game.

The rules of the game are simple: flip the cards over and match the emojis correctly as quickly as possible.

Also, make sure to get as little incorrect pairings to get as many stars as possible

First, choose a difficulty level. The harder the difficulty, the quicker the stars will dissappear as incorrect pairings are chosen.
