Welcome to Concentration: the classic matching game.

The rules are simple: find all matching pairs of cards before you run out of stars. The difficulty level you choose will determine how many misses it takes to lose your stars, so choose wisely!

Choose Difficulty:

Behind this screen there are sixteen face-down cards, each with a secret emoji on the back. Click on a pair of cards to see if they match. If you find all the matching cards while retaining some stars, the game is over and you win!

Compete against your friends and yourself to try to complete the game in the least amount of moves, on the hardest difficulty, in the least amount of time!

Once you are ready, press 'Begin' and get matching!
